📢 PathForge Updates

PathForge is continuously evolving to provide a complete career preparation experience — from structured learning to real-world interview readiness.

✅ Completed Features

  • Role-Based Roadmap System:
    • Implemented multiple career paths including Frontend, Data Analyst, UI/UX, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, and more
    • Each role contains a structured and logical learning sequence
  • Structured Learning Flow:
    • Designed step-by-step roadmap from beginner to advanced level
    • Ensures users follow a clear and organized path
  • Topic-Based Navigation:
    • Users can click on individual topics within a roadmap
    • Each topic opens detailed content and interview preparation
  • Interview Preparation Module:
    • Added interview questions for each subject
    • Includes both fresher-level and core-level questions
  • Real Interview Answer Approach:
    • Answers are designed in a practical, human-speaking format
    • Avoids textbook-style explanations
  • User Interface Design:
    • Implemented a clean and modern dark-themed UI
    • Focused on simplicity, readability, and user experience
  • Updates / Changelog Page:
    • Created a dedicated page to track development progress
    • Improves transparency and product feel

🚀 Upcoming Features (In Progress)

  • Continuous Content Expansion:
    • Regular updates to roadmaps based on industry trends
    • More interview questions and improved answer quality

  • Voice-Based Interview Practice:

    • System will evaluate responses and provide:

    • Advanced feature to analyze real interview behavior

  • Enhanced Interview Experience:
    • More realistic interview simulations
    • Improved question flow and practice environment
  • UI/UX Improvements:
    • Transition to a more premium, product-level interface
